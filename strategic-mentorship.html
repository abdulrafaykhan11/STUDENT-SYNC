CS vs SE: what should I choose?

CS gives you a wider computing foundation; SE puts more emphasis on building and managing software systems. Choose based on curriculum, projects and where you want to work—not title alone.

Ayesha says: “Look at the first four semesters. Strong programming and problem-solving matter more than the label.”

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Start early: collect transcripts, income documents, a short personal statement, and references. Track each deadline in one sheet and apply even when you are unsure.

Mariam says: “Your story should show effort and impact, not just need.”

How should I prepare for an entry test?

Use timed practice twice a week, log every mistake by topic, then revise weak topics before doing another full test.

Dr. Hamza says: “Consistency beats last-month cramming—review mistakes the same day.”
